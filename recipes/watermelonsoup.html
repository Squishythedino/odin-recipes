Watermelon Soup

Description

A refreshing soup, perfect for the summer! Combined watermelon and lemon juice compliment each other perfectly.

Try adding salt for a kick!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Gather everything
  2. Blend everything together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 hours, serve chilled.
