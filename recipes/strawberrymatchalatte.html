Strawberry Matcha Latte

Description

The drink of your summer, the Strawberry Matcha Latte. Strawberries are in season this summer and that makes them taste better.

Add some farm fresh strawberries to this drink and enjoy the taste of summer.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup finely chopped fresh strawberries (roughly 4 to 5 strawberries)
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • ice as needed
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened milk of choice

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries and sugar into a bowl, use fork to lightly crush and mix.
  2. Spoon into glass.
  3. Whisk boiling water and matcha powder in a cup until well combined.
  4. Top crushed strawberry mix with ice. Slowly pour milk over it and then follow by the matcha.
  5. Stir and serve with a wide straw.
