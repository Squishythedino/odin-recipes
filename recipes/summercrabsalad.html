Summer Crab Salad

Description

Crab is the symbol of summer I fear, and what better way of celebrating that then by eating it.

Try it with imitation crab, I'm curious!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh crab meat
  • 1/3 cup dill pickles, chopped
  • 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped red onions
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tablespoon cocktail sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Mix the crabmeat, pickles, tomatoes, red onions, lemon juice, mayonnaise, coctail sauce, and garlic powder in a bowl and serve.
  2. That's it!
